## Hypnotic Techno Production Tips

### Tempo & Timing
- **Range:** 125-132 BPM (BCCO: 126-128)
- **Swing:** 40-50% on hi-hats for hypnotic groove
- **Don't over-quantize** - leave pocket feel

### Rhythm Foundation
- **Kick:** 4-on-floor with ghost kicks on 1.2.3, 1.3.2
- **Hi-Hat:** 16th notes with swing
- **Bass:** Rolling 16ths with subtle bar-to-bar variation
- **Crest Factor:** 6-10dB target

### Sound Design
- **Bass:** FM synthesis (3-operator setup + sine sub)
- **Pads:** Wavetable with long reverb (2-4s decay)
- **Drums:** Punchy, minimal, tight compression
- **Texture:** Subtle filtered noise, modulated

### Frequency Targets (Hypnotic Techno)
| Range | Hz | Target dB |
|-------|-----|-----------|
| Sub | 20-60 | 30-35 |
| Bass | 60-250 | 25-30 |
| Low-Mid | 250-500 | 20-25 |
| Mid | 500-2k | 15-25 |
| Upper-Mid | 2k-4k | 10-20 |
| Presence | 4k-6k | 5-15 |
| Brilliance | 6k-12k | 0-10 |
| Air | 12k-20k | -10 to 5 |

### Mixing
- High-pass at 20Hz
- Narrow Q cuts for problems
- Avoid boosting - prefer gentle shelving
- **Sidechain:** Kick triggers bass (6dB reduction typical)

### Arrangement Structure
- **Intro (32 bars):** Kick + filtered bass
- **Building (32 bars):** Add element every 16 bars
- **Main (64+ bars):** Full arrangement, subtle modulation
- **Breakdown (32 bars):** Remove bass, keep kick + texture
- **Build (32-64 bars):** Gradual reintroduction
- **Outro (32 bars):** Fade to kick or silence

### Mastering
- **Target:** -6 to -8dB RMS, peaks ~-1dB
- **LUFS:** -7 to -8 integrated
- **Limiting:** Soft clip at -0.3dB
